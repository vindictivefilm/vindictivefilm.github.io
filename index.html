What is Vindictive?

Vindictive is a terrifying journey into the supernatural, a mad dash of cinematic chaos. Like rats trapped in a maze, the teens fight for survival against this vengeful crazed teacher's ghost.

From the demented minds of Joe Ritter, Ken Fleischer and Simon Rubenstein, Vindictive is the heart-pounding story of Mrs. Maliss, the ghost of a 1930's Brooklyn School Teacher who traps 6 present day teenage girls and one teenage boy in her classroom of horror behind the walls of an old Brooklyn Apartment Building.
